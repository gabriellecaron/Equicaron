The structure ... The equestrian center extends over an area of 15 hectares: - 2 covered riding arenas -1 Olympic carousel of 60m X 20m - 1 Shetland riding arena of 18m diameter - 4 careers - 1 shetland quarry - 1 pony quarry of 40m X 20m - 1 dressage arena of 60m X 20m - 1 obstacle course of 60m X 50m - 54 boxes - cross-country course - numerous footpaths with access to the beach - 6 paddocks - club house - classroom

The spirit of the equestrian center ... - EQUI CARON aims above all to develop common values around the horse: - mutual aid, - the learning of autonomy, - A friendly atmosphere - the respect of the horse and the other. - EQUICARON transmits its ideals through : - its teachings, - his riding lessons from beginner to advanced, - outing in competition, - theme days, - walks, - professional training. - EQUICARON is more than a riding school! It is a meeting with a qualified team, passionate riders and endearing horses. - Riding lessons are given from the age of 3 years old with courses adapted to the very young!
