WELCOME ON EQUICARON

Located only 500m from the sea, Equi-CARON's equestrian center welcomes you all year round to help you discover the joys of horseback riding at any age, while respecting the horse.

Also a training center for the future instructors of tomorrow, its experience and seriousness will ensure you a quality training geared towards your professional integration.

Since the summer of 2020, EQUI-CARON also offers horse therapy for people with physical and neurological disabilities, with horses taken in in our refuge. We recover them when they are abandoned and mistreated to offer them the life they deserve, in peace and serenity.
